We believe that providing good mental health services starts with a healthy workplace.

FLEXIBLE

We offer a work environment that’s fully flexible and remote, allowing you to choose how much you work, when you work and where you work. All therapy sessions are taken online from home at your convenience.

THERAPY CENTRIC

QUALITIES WE SEEK

At The Friendly Couch, we select psychologists who share our vision. Our ideal team member is:

  • APPROPRIATELY QUALIFIED
    You must have studied Psychology academically for at least 5 years i.e. completed your Bachelors and Masters Degrees in Psychology to work with us.
  • EMPATHETIC
    You should possess the ability to understand and connect with the emotions and experiences of clients, creating a safe and supportive environment for therapy.
  • PROFESSIONAL AND ETHICAL
    You should maintain professional boundaries, adhere to ethical standards, and respect the client’s interests.
  • OPEN MINDED
    You should accept feedback and show willingness to respect cultural differences.
