OUR COUNSELLING PROCESS

INDIVIDUAL SESSION

We start by working with each partner individually to explore their emotions, perspectives, and experiences in depth. This helps us gain a comprehensive understanding of both sides of the relationship.

JOINT SESSION

Building on the insights from individual sessions, we bring both partners together for the core of the therapeutic process. These sessions encourage open dialogue, provide exercises, and focus on improving communication, understanding, and emotional connection.

PERSONAL SESSION

After the initial round of sessions (two individual and one joint), our therapist will create a personalised plan. This may involve more individual sessions or regular joint sessions, depending on the couple's needs and progress.
