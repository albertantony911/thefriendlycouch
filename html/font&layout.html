What we do

Mental health awareness has increased but finding a therapist can be a difficult task. There are so many different types of professionals (and quacks) in the field, how does one know who to choose?

That is where The Friendly Couch helps. We select and work with therapists that are non-judgmental, have the right qualifications, and most importantly, are friendly.

Because who would want to go to an intimidating therapist you can’t connect to, right?
