  • A place to share your thoughts and feelings without judgement.
  • An alliance that offers insights into your emotions and behaviours.

OUR PROCESS

REACH OUT

When you're ready to start therapy, simply drop us a message on our Instagram or WhatsApp. Our team will guide and help you find a suitable therapist. Feel free to let us know your preferences (if any).

BOOK YOUR APPOINTMENT

Once connected with a suitable therapist on our team, you can choose a convenient slot for your session with them and confirm your appointment as directed by the team. For your convenience, you can securely make your payment online during this step.

PRE-SESSION PROCESS

Once your appointment is confirmed, a consent form will be shared by the therapist to help you understand our policies and your rights as a client of The Friendly Couch. You will also receive a video call link for your session once the consent form has been signed.

SESSION

On the scheduled date of your appointment, the therapist will conduct your session to understand your concerns and create a treatment plan with you.
